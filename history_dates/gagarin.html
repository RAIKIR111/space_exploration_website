April 12, 1961 - Yuri Gagarin became the first human to journey into outer space, achieving a major goal in the Space Race. His capsule Vostok 1 completed one orbit of Earth on 12 April 1961. Gagarin became an international celebrity and was awarded many medals and titles, including Hero of the Soviet Union, his nation's highest honour.
